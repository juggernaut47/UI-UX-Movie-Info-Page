INTERSTELLAR

Mankind was born on Earth. It was never meant to die here.

  • 2014 • Sci‑Fi, Adventure, Drama
  • Directed by Christopher Nolan
  • Starring Matthew McConaughey, Anne Hathaway, Jessica Chastain

Plot Summary

When Earth becomes uninhabitable, a former pilot is recruited for a last‑chance mission: travel through a newly discovered wormhole to find a new home among the stars. Along the way, the crew confronts black holes, time dilation, and the ultimate test of love across dimensions.
